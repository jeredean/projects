Key Concepts

Understanding the foundational concepts behind LLMs is essential for making sense of their capabilities and limitations. From tokens and context windows to safety and alignment, these ideas shape how models process information, generate responses, and interact with users. Explore these key concepts to deepen your knowledge of modern AI.
